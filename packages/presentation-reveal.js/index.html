Music in general is healthy

  • improves creativity
  • reduces stress (by lowering cortisol level)
  • improves quality of live, e.g. recreation, dancing
  • can even heal diseases e.g. depression, PTSD, insomnia
  • touches a lot of districts in our brain
  • makes our brain fire neurons that create new synapses

Not only playing, but also consuming music is extremely engaging.
Although not every music is the same .... We'll come to this later.

  • PTSD == post traumatic stress disorder

Benefits of learning an instrument

(in general)

Music produces brain-building compounds and neural growth factors that are making you smarter, stronger and better at doing activities throughout the day.

Brain

  • Spatial skils
  • Auditory processing skills
  • Motor skills
  • helps learn other things easier
  • Spatial skils e.g. problem solving, arranging items, connecting pieces, or doing puzzles
  • Auditory processing skills, e.g. ability to remember things that were heard or ability to distinguish sounds
  • Motor skills e.g. better hand-eye coordination or balancing skills
  • Both hemispheres involved helps learn other things easier e.g. languages, math
