Music in general is healthy

  • improves creativity
  • reduces stress (by lowering cortisol level)
  • improves quality of live, e.g. recreation, dancing
  • can even heal diseases e.g. depression, PTSD, insomnia
  • touches a lot of districts in our brain
  • makes our brain fire neurons that create new synapses

Not only playing, but also consuming music is extremely engaging.
Although not every music is the same .... We'll come to this later.

Benefits of learning an instrument

(in general)

"... strong cognitive stimulus that grows the brain in a way that nothing else does..."
Music produces brain-building compounds and neural growth factors that are making you smarter, stronger and better at doing activities throughout the day.

Brain

  • Spatial skils
  • Auditory processing skills
  • Motor skills
  • helps learn other things easier
  • Spatial skils e.g. problem solving, arranging items, connecting pieces or doing puzzles
  • Auditory processing skills, e.g. ability to remember things that were heard or ability to distinguish sounds
  • Motor skills e.g. better hand-eye coordination or balancing skills
  • Both hemispheres involved and connected helps learn other things easier e.g. languages, math
"If I were not a physicist, I would probably be a musician. I often think in music. I live my daydreams in music." (Albert Einstein)

emotional and personal development

  • cultural and historical relation with music
  • self confession, expression and confidence
  • empathy and emotional development
  • exercising empowers discipline
  • music let's you learn more about the history / circumstances of the music piece you play
  • instrument learners are evolving higher self esteem and slef confidence especially if they start performing and achieving their goals
  • emotional interpretation of music teaches empathy implicitely
  • perseverance, discipline and responsibility

Social

  • teamwork
  • new friends
  • relationships
  • likeability (**)
  • even in different environments e.g. work
  • whether it is with choir, band etc. or in concerts
  • The training that the mind and body go through to develop musical comprehension is the same as the training required to develop speech and verbal skills, essential components in any relationship.
